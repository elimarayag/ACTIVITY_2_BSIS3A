Arctic Monkeys

   Arctic Monkeys are an English rock band formed in Sheffield in 2002. With their nervy and literate indie rock sound, Arctic Monkeys arrived with a blast in 2005. Assisted by rave reviews and online word of mouth (they were one of the first bands to benefit from social media), they quickly became a sensation in the United Kingdom.
